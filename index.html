BMI calculation helps determine if an individual is in a healthy weight range. It is calculated using the formula BMI = Weight (kg) / Height (m²). A normal BMI falls between 18.5 and 24.9, which indicates a healthy weight range for an individual.

For most people, BMI provides a good measure of obesity. However, it doesn’t give specific details on body composition, like muscle or fat percentages. Individuals with higher muscle mass may fall into an "overweight" category despite being healthy. Thus, BMI should be considered carefully based on individual circumstances.
